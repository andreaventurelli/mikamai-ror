Ruby on Rails

RoR is an open-source platform for web development based on the Ruby language. Its neatness and the availability of an extended library of methods and plugins makes the development of websites an activity that is easy, dramatically effective and also fun.

Why we love RoR

  • Open source
  • Full-stack
  • DRY
  • TDD

Why you'll love RoR

  • Open source -> Always up to date
  • Full-stack -> The development of the frontend and backend part can proceed as a whole, organically and efficiently
  • DRY -> Software development is quick and effective, for our developers and for you, too
  • TDD -> consistency and structure in coding practices mean that it is easy and fast to make changes to existing code, add new features etc.
